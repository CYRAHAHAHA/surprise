## Tips

- Use high-quality images (1920x1080 or larger recommended)
- Soft, romantic images work best (flowers, bokeh, soft gradients)
- The app adds a subtle overlay for text readability
- Supported formats: `.jpg`, `.png`, `.webp`

## Ideas for Images

- Soft pink/purple gradient backgrounds
- Blurry bokeh lights
- Rose petals
- Sunset/sunrise skies
- Your favorite photos together (blurred for background use)
